About Me

Theo Kwan, better known by his stage name tall du$ty, is an independent content creator, singer, songwriter, and rapper from the Pacific Northwest. He dropped his debut single “Rocks Really Rock” in February 2021; and followed with educational ballads “The Qudratic Formula” and “Hugo Chávez” on which he collaborated with fellow YouTuber and rapper yung_edgar. Dusty released his first full length album “TALL DUSTY AND THE SKELETON CREW” in October 2021.
